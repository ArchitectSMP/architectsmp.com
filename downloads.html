Instructions

Make a new singleplayer world, load it and then close it again.
Go to where your minecraft files are installed:

OS Location
Windows %APPDATA%\.minecraft.
MacOS ~/Library/Application Support/minecraft
Linux ~/.minecraft
1. Open the saves folder, find the world you just created and open the folder.
2. Delete all files within the folder
3. Go to the ArchitectSMP Season zip file, open it or unpack it to access the contents.
4. Open the world folder, copy everything EXCEPT DIM1 and DIM-1 and paste it into your folder.
5. Go back to the ArchitectSMP Season zip file and open world_nether.
6. Copy the contents of DIM-1 and paste int into your folder.
7. Go back to the ArchitectSMP Season zip file and open world_end.
6. Copy the contents of DIM1 and paste int into your folder.

You can now close all folders and return to your launcher.
In the singleplayer menu the Season world is called "world".

Happy exploring!
